Where is Lelo from?

Lelo was born in Mothibistad at the Kagiso Clinic. Since then, she has been raised in Mothibistad and Magojaneng from the year 2004 to the year 2008. Then after the seperation of her parents, she was in between Mothibistad and Kuruman town most of the time. She would occassionally visit her mother's home in a small village in Maphinick, which is situatued in the surrounding villages of Kuruman.

During her first few years of high school, she attended boarding school in Kimberley, Northern Cape at the Kumberley Technical High School and then moved back to Kuruman for the last two years of high school.
